Flash Exhibits

The Flash Exhibits unfold in the heart of the Art Fair square, offering spontaneous bursts of visual storytelling and creative energy. These pop-up showcases amplify the fair’s immersive spirit—inviting visitors to encounter unexpected moments of art, beauty, and connection as they explore.

We intend with these exhibits to create a dynamic space about creativity and community, where photographers share their work with others, making it a constant celebration.
